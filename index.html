Who We Are

About DevKrupa Engineering Works

Established in 2000, DevKrupa Engineering Works is a leading service provider in precision engineering and machining, headquartered in Indore, Madhya Pradesh.

As a proprietor-led firm, we have built a solid reputation for delivering high-quality gear shafts, gear pinions, CNC machined precision parts, and bespoke component development that our clients can always rely on.

Our key clients include Eicher, Dewas Components, and Hindustan Precision Works, reflecting our unwavering commitment to delivering reliable, quality solutions to respected industry names.

With ISO 9001 certification, we ensure international standards of quality and consistency in every part we manufacture. All our products undergo rigorous quality checks before delivery.
